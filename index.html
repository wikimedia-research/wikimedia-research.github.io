Wikimedia Research

Wikimedia Foundation's research and data analysis code can be found on GitHub (here) and on GitLab (repos/research, repos/product-analytics).

The Wikimedia Research Newsletter (WRN) is a joint initiative of the Wikimedia Research Committee and the Signpost to cover research updates of relevance to the Wikimedia community. The newsletter is edited monthly and features both internal research at the Wikimedia Foundation and work conducted by external research teams.

Refer to MediaWiki documentation for more information about the Product Analytics team. Refer to research.wikimedia.org for more information about the Research team.

Follow @WikiResearch or @wikiresearch@mastodon.social for updates.
